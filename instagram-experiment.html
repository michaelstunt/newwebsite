August 2021 - November 2021

I made 67 Instagram post graphics with famous quotes. Over the course of three months the account I posted these quotes on accumulated a peak count of 1908 followers.
‍
While I believe if I were to produce Instagram content again I could comfortably pass a follower count of 1908 in three months, that was not the point of this project.
‍
This project was focused on ending my growing habit of beginning a project then continually planning without actually creating anything. Instead, I decided I ought to practice just settling for 'good-enough' and hitting share. Hence, a lot of these posts are rather mediocre. Nonetheless, I learnt the value of just doing and being comfortable with being not perfect.
