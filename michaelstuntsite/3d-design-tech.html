This is a fairly amusing project to be placed in my portfolio simply because it was made for my GCSE's - something I (like everyone else) seem like an ancient time away. I still choose to place this among my other projects because its been my one opportunity to commit to physical drawing for an extended period of time. So while the written commentary and annotations of my work are fairly bland and comically simple at times, the resulting illustrations are drawings I am still proud of today.
